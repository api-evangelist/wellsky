The National Core Indicators for Aging and Disabilities (NCI-AD™) is a voluntary effort by State Medicaid, aging, and disability agencies to measure and track the outcomes of services provided to individuals and families. The latest two reports were just released: the Adult Consumer Survey and the State of the Workforce for Aging and Disabilities. Join WellSky for a webinar on the key findings.
